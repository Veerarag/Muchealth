WHAT DO YOU NEED TO LOOK OUT FOR?
    Most people with high blood pressure have no signs or symptoms, even if blood pressure readings reach dangerously high levels. Therefore, it is important to get your BP checked regularly at home for precaution. These symptoms are not specific and usually occurs when blood pressure levels have reach severe or life-threatening stages
  1. Are you experiencing severe headaches?
  2. Are you having frequent nosebleeds?
  3. Are you experiencing difficulties in breathing?
  4. Did you notice any blood in your urine?
  5. Are you having any sudden or frequent onset of chest pain?
  6. Are you having any blurring of vision?
  7. Are you experiencing any numbness over your hands or feet?
